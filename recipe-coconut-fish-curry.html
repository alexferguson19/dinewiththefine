Coconut Fish Curry

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 thumb piece ginger
  • 2 red chillies
  • 1 bunch coriander
  • 1 small onion, quartered
  • 2 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 2-3 tbsp water
  • 550g firm fleshed fish (salmon recommended)
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • Chilli oil, herbs, and fried shallots (for toppings)

Instructions

1. Place a wok or pan over medium heat and add mustard seeds. Toast until they pop then add ghee (or butter).
2. Add curry paste and turn up the heat, stirring for 1-2 minutes.
3. Add coconut milk, stir well, and simmer for 5 minutes.
4. Add fish pieces and cook for 3-4 minutes until just cooked through.
5. Season with soy sauce, lime juice, and sugar. Adjust as desired.
6. Transfer to a dish, drizzle with chili oil, and top with herbs and fried shallots.
